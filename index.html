Wookiefoot

Be Fearless and Play

Wookiefoot is not only a band, but also a performance group. Their performances include music, light shows, aerialists, fire, and more. While Mark Murphy (Lead vocals, guitar) and Jay Lash (Background vocals, hypeman) are the main members, the group always has different members. The members of Wookiefoot and many other individuals work together to raise money for their charity, Be the Change. This goal of this organization is to help out those in need and also to save the Earth. Wookiefoot holds two festivals a year in southern Minnesota in order to raise money for this their charity.

The music of Wookiefoot revolves around positivity. The bands works to spread the message of kindness to anyone willing to listen. Their music has helped many people out of terrible mental situations.
